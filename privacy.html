At Beinganimelover, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. By using our site, you agree to the terms outlined here.

01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit Beinganimelover:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, and referral sources — collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored in your browser to improve your experience.
  • Voluntarily Provided: If you contact us via email or social media, we may retain that communication.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or payment details unless you explicitly provide them.

02

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website and improve content accordingly.
  • Monitor and fix technical issues or errors.
  • Analyze traffic patterns to improve user experience.
  • Respond to inquiries or feedback you send us.

We never sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

03

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand visitor behavior through tools like Google Analytics.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember settings like theme or language preferences.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

04

We embed content from third-party platforms including:

  • YouTube: Video embeds may place cookies from Google/YouTube on your device. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: Used to analyze website traffic anonymously.
  • Google Fonts & CDN Libraries: Used to load fonts and scripts efficiently.
05

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to know what data we have about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to opt out of analytics tracking.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.
06

Beinganimelover is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.
